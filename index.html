Hi!

My name is Matheus Diógenes Andrade, and currently, I am a master’s degree student in computer science at the Institute of Computing (IC) at the University of Campinas (Unicamp).

Before that, I coursed a bachelor in software engineering at the Federal University of Ceará campus Russas, supervised by Professor Ph.D. Dmontier Aragão and co-supervised by Professor Ramom Santana Rebouças, I developed a particular interest in optimization and combinatorics. After completing my bachelor’s, I joined an MSc with the supervision of my advisor Professor Ph.D. Fábio Usberti, which I am currently enrolled in.
